# InsightApp Product Backlog

This file contains features and improvements that are planned for future development but not part of the current MVP.

## Core Features (Future Releases)

### Candidate Discovery Features
- **Smart Matching Algorithm**: AI-powered matching between candidates and job requirements
- **Candidate Profile Visibility Controls**: Allow candidates to control who can see their profiles
- **Application Tracking**: Let candidates track which employers have viewed their profiles
- **Profile Analytics**: Show candidates how their profiles are performing
- **Multi-language Support**: Support for recording in different languages
- **Profile Sharing**: Allow candidates to share profile links directly with employers

### Advanced Recording Features
- **Guided Interview Prompts**: AI-generated personalized questions based on resume
- **Multiple Recording Sessions**: Allow candidates to add multiple recordings over time
- **Video Recording Option**: Support for video recordings in addition to audio
- **Live Transcription**: Real-time transcription during recording
- **Recording Quality Assessment**: AI feedback on recording quality and suggestions for improvement
- **Practice Mode**: Let candidates practice before final recording

### Employer Advanced Features
- **Custom Query Templates**: Save and reuse common search queries
- **Candidate Comparison Tool**: Side-by-side comparison of multiple candidates
- **Team Collaboration**: Share candidate profiles with team members and collect feedback
- **Interview Scheduling Integration**: Direct integration with calendar systems
- **ATS Integration**: Connect with existing Applicant Tracking Systems
- **Bulk Actions**: Save, export, or contact multiple candidates at once
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by salary range, availability, location preferences, etc.
- **Candidate Ranking**: AI-powered ranking based on job requirements

### AI Enhancements
- **Personality Insights**: AI analysis of communication style and cultural fit
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identify areas where candidates might need development
- **Interview Question Generation**: Personalized interview questions based on candidate background
- **Bias Detection**: AI monitoring to reduce unconscious bias in candidate evaluation
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Analyze enthusiasm and passion in recordings
- **Technical Depth Assessment**: AI evaluation of technical knowledge depth

### Platform Features
- **Mobile App**: Native iOS and Android apps for recording and browsing
- **Real-time Notifications**: Notify candidates when employers view their profiles
- **Profile Versioning**: Track changes to candidate profiles over time
- **Export Options**: PDF, Word, and other format exports of candidate profiles
- **API Access**: Allow third-party integrations
- **White-label Solution**: Customizable platform for enterprise clients

### Analytics & Insights
- **Employer Analytics Dashboard**: Track hiring success rates, time-to-hire, etc.
- **Candidate Success Metrics**: Track which profiles get the most attention
- **Market Insights**: Salary trends, skill demand, location preferences
- **Diversity Metrics**: Track diversity in candidate pipeline and hiring
- **ROI Calculator**: Show employers time and cost savings

### Quality & Trust Features
- **Skill Verification**: Optional skill assessments to verify candidate claims
- **Reference Integration**: Connect with professional references
- **Background Check Integration**: Partner with background check services
- **Fake Profile Detection**: AI detection of inconsistent or fake information
- **Candidate Verification**: Email and phone verification processes

## Priority Framework

**P0 (Critical)**: Essential for MVP functionality
**P1 (High)**: Important for user adoption and retention
**P2 (Medium)**: Nice-to-have features that improve user experience
**P3 (Low)**: Future innovation and experimental features

Most items in this backlog are P2-P3 priority, focusing on the current MVP development first.
